When you think about traveling to Spain, what is the first thing that comes to your mind? Yes, we already know the answer ... Spanish omelette! Well, we'd better start calling it properly: "Tortilla".

It is everywhere, all bars and restaurants have them on their menus, they even have tortilla sandwiches ...crazy, right? To be honest, we were a little skeptical about trying it but it turns out it was a fantastic discovery! Anyway, we have tried the best and the "not so good" ones and, as eating delicious food puts us in a good mood, we're going to share every place we discovered with you so...
